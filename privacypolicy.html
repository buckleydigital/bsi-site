Privacy Policy

Better Solar Installers Australia — How we handle your information

Last updated: March 2025

1. Who We Are

Better Solar Installers Australia ("we", "us", "our") operates as a solar and battery installer referral matching service. We connect Australian homeowners with pre-vetted, licensed and insured solar installation companies ("Installers") in their local area.

We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

For any privacy-related enquiries, contact us at: contact@bettersolarinstallers.online

2. What Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request through our website, we collect the following personal information:

  • First and last name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address
  • Suburb, city and state
  • Your preferences regarding solar and/or battery installation
  • Your estimated installation timeline
  • Property type

We may also automatically collect non-identifying technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, for the purposes of website analytics and improving our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Match you with a suitable, pre-vetted local solar or battery Installer
  • Pass your contact details and requirements to your matched Installer so they can contact you with an obligation-free quote
  • Communicate with you regarding your quote request
  • Improve our website and referral service
  • Comply with our legal obligations
Important: By submitting a quote request, you consent to being contacted by phone, text and/or email by Better Solar Installers Australia and/or your matched Installer regarding your enquiry.

4. Sharing Your Information

We will share your personal information with your matched Installer for the sole purpose of providing you with a solar or battery installation quote. This is the core function of our service.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with unrelated marketers or advertisers.

We may also share your information where required by law, court order, or government authority.

We use third-party service providers (such as CRM and automation platforms) to operate our business. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies, including the Meta Pixel (Facebook Pixel), to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and improve our service. This may include tracking whether you have visited our website or submitted a form.

You can opt out of interest-based advertising through your browser settings or by visiting youronlinechoices.com.au.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your personal information is stored securely using reputable third-party platforms. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access.

Our data may be stored on servers located outside Australia (for example, in the United States or European Union). Where this occurs, we take steps to ensure that the overseas recipient handles your information consistently with the APPs.

7. Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or out-of-date information
  • Opt out of direct marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at contact@bettersolarinstallers.online.

8. Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our service and comply with legal obligations. If you request deletion of your data, we will action this within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required by law to retain it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on our website. Continued use of our service following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy questions or complaints, please contact:

Better Solar Installers Australia
Email: contact@bettersolarinstallers.online

We will respond to all enquiries within 30 days.
